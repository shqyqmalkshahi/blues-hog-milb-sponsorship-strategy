# Blues Hog BBQ × Minor League Baseball (MiLB) Sponsorship Strategy

##  Overview
This project develops a data-driven sponsorship strategy for Blues Hog BBQ using Minor League Baseball (MiLB) as a targeted marketing channel.

The analysis focuses on leveraging existing distribution, optimizing in-stadium engagement, and identifying scalable sponsorship opportunities through ownership structures.

---

##  Problem Statement
Traditional sponsorships often focus on brand awareness without ensuring product availability or conversion. This project addresses how Blues Hog can align sponsorship efforts with real distribution and purchasing behavior to maximize impact.

---

##  Objectives
- Identify high-value MiLB markets for sponsorship
- Align sponsorship strategy with existing distribution channels
- Evaluate ownership structures for scalable expansion
- Assess concession opportunities for direct consumer engagement
- Optimize sponsorship activation for measurable impact

---

##  Approach & Methodology
Markets were evaluated using a structured framework including:

- Ownership structure (scalability potential)
- Distribution alignment (retail & foodservice presence)
- Concession opportunities (sampling and menu integration)
- Sponsorship inventory (visibility and exposure)
- Execution feasibility (ease of activation)

---

##  Key Insights
- MiLB provides strong community-based engagement with frequent exposure
- Concession integration is the most effective driver of brand interaction
- Sponsorships are most valuable when aligned with existing distribution
- Ownership groups enable faster and more scalable expansion compared to independent teams
- Combining brands (e.g., Blues Hog + Good2Grow) increases overall sponsorship value

---

##  Strategic Recommendations
- Start with a focused pilot in high-leverage markets
- Prioritize concession-based activations for direct product exposure
- Expand through ownership groups for scalability
- Align sponsorship with retail distribution to drive conversion, not just awareness

---

##  Business Value
This project demonstrates how data-driven decision-making can transform sponsorship strategy from awareness-based marketing into a revenue-aligned growth initiative.
