# Blues Hog BBQ × Minor League Baseball (MiLB) Sponsorship Strategy

## Overview
This project develops a data-driven sponsorship strategy for Blues Hog BBQ using Minor League Baseball (MiLB) as a targeted marketing channel. The analysis focuses on aligning sponsorship investments with real-world distribution, maximizing in-stadium engagement, and identifying scalable expansion opportunities.

---

## Problem Statement
Traditional sponsorship strategies often prioritize brand awareness without ensuring product availability or measurable conversion. This creates a disconnect between marketing investment and actual revenue impact.

This project addresses how Blues Hog can align sponsorship efforts with distribution, consumption behavior, and operational feasibility to maximize effectiveness.

---

## Objective
To design a scalable sponsorship strategy that:

- Targets high-value MiLB markets  
- Aligns sponsorships with existing distribution channels  
- Leverages ownership structures for expansion  
- Integrates products into concessions for direct engagement  
- Maximizes measurable impact beyond brand awareness  

---

## Methodology

Markets were evaluated using a structured decision framework including:

- **Ownership Structure** → scalability potential across teams  
- **Distribution Alignment** → existing retail and foodservice presence  
- **Concession Opportunities** → sampling and menu integration  
- **Sponsorship Inventory** → visibility and exposure options  
- **Execution Feasibility** → ease of activation and rollout  

This framework ensures decisions are data-driven and scalable rather than subjective.

---

## Key Insights

- MiLB provides strong community-based engagement with frequent exposure  
- Concession integration is the most effective driver of brand interaction  
- Sponsorships are most valuable when aligned with existing distribution  
- Ownership groups enable faster expansion compared to independent teams  
- Combining brands (e.g., Blues Hog + Good2Grow) increases total sponsorship value  

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- Launch a focused pilot in high-leverage MiLB markets  
- Prioritize concession-based activations for direct product exposure  
- Expand through ownership groups to scale efficiently  
- Align sponsorship efforts with retail distribution to drive conversion  

---

## Execution Strategy

The recommended approach follows a phased model:

1. **Pilot Phase**
   - Select 1–2 high-impact markets  
   - Focus on concession integration and visibility  

2. **Validation Phase**
   - Measure engagement and conversion impact  

3. **Expansion Phase**
   - Scale through ownership networks  
   - Expand into adjacent markets

This strategy transforms sponsorship from a brand-awareness activity into a revenue-aligned growth initiative by:
